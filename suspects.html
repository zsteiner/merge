The process begins with flagging suspected duplicates. Percent match shows the overlap of key demographics. 100% matches are separate records with identical information. These are safe to merge immediately without resolving differences.

The user can merge all 100% matches at the click of a button because this is a low risk activity for data integrity standpoint. However, offering forgiveness is important after this action by allowing the user to split (undo) all of the records or some sub-set of records.
